Project Name: Media Campaign Analysis 

Project Overview: 
This project includes datasets based on the advertisment campaigns that has been run on the CPG products from Walmart. There are two data sources: Facebook Data and Site Data, each of which are vital to telling the complete picture of campaign performance. 

Tools: Excel( Pivot Tables, V-lookUp, 

Organisation : Vayner Media LLC

Datasets: Facebook Ads Data & Site Data

KPIs: 

- Cost Per Video Completion (($)) = Media Spend/Video Views (Upto 95%)

- CPM  (Media Spend Per 1000 Impressions)

- ROAS (Return On Advertisment Spend)

- CTR (Clicks/Impressions)

- CPC  (Media Spend/Number of Clicks)

Insights:

  -  The Evergreen and Valentine’s Day campaigns collectively drove a significant media investment of $165,616.41, indicating strong strategic emphasis on always-on presence complemented by seasonal demand capture.This level of spend suggests confidence in these campaigns’ ability to sustain brand visibility while capitalizing on high-intent seasonal traffic.

  - The Free Shipping video creatives delivered strong engagement efficiency, with a low Cost per Video View ($0.04) and Cost per Completion ($0.31), demonstrating high audience resonance and effective messaging.

  - Brand Logo creatives with a “Save Now” CTA achieved the most efficient CPM, highlighting strong algorithmic favorability and cost-effective reach at the awareness stage. This suggests that simple, brand-forward messaging paired with a clear incentive performs well in driving efficient impressions.

  - The following solutions focuses on deep diving and further segmenting certain attributes in a step by step manner to optimise Evergreen campaigns using Pivot Table options. The conclusions from the analysis can be explained as follows:- 

      1) Optimization Chanel Analysis:
           - An additional attribute, Profit Per Chanel ($) = [Total Revenues-Total Media Spend] has been taken as a basis to decide which Optimization Event needs more focus. 
           - The Conversion Event generates the maximum ad profit of $ 207,784, indicating that we can further optimize our Evergreen Campaign by deep diving into the factors that led the audience to 
             become the customers.

      2) Audience Type Analysis:
          - The pivot table analyses the target audience , that had been converted under the Evergreen campaign ads based upon the ROAS (Return On Ad Spend).
          - Website Custom Audience has generated the maximum ROAS of $ 2,042 using the campaign ads called "Brand Logo + Save Now CTA".
          - The following analysis indicates a future potential for more media spend to induce Website Custom Audience to bring in more future campaings using "Brand Logo + Save Now CTA".

      3) Campaign Traffic Analysis:
          - The following table analyses the traffic that can be  potentially generated by the audience that strongly that strongly relates with the "Website Custom Audience"  that based on average number of clicks generated on the post type.
          - The Home Page has been observed to generate the maximum campaign traffic through the average number of clicks through Video ad posts, which is generating the average number of clicks of 58.
          - After further deep diving into Video ads for further analysis, majority of the audience under the campaign has the viewership completion of upto 25% of the whole video length. Indicating the potential to put more emphasis on short reels as a medium for maximumsing ads traffic.

      4) ROAS forecasting:
         This analysis validates the impact of the Shopping Cart Page on revenue and forecasts future performance.
          - The Shopping Cart Page generated a maximum daily revenue of $5,280 in February.
          - Forecasting Method: This forecast was determined by analyzing the trend using the average Daily Growth Rate (%DAGR) of the campaign performance. The forecast targeted the first day of the March month.
          - Positive Campaign Impact: The last day of the campaign (February 28th, 2017) showed an impressive increase of upto $51.87.
          - Conclusion: The positive trend confirms that the Shopping Cart Page is a viable and scalable resource for future marketing campaigns.
